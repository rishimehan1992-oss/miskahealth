# MISKA Backlink Playbook

Goal: earn **quality** links to product and journal pages. Log everything in `seo/backlinks.json`.

## Link-worthy assets (pitch these)

1. **Ingredient deep-dives** — `/products/*/ingredients`
2. **Journal posts** — science-forward, not salesy
3. **Homepage** — brand story + clinic formulation angle (harder to place)

## Outreach templates

### Beauty editor / listicle

Subject: MISKA clinical hair actives — data for "best hair fall products India" lists

> Hi [Name],
>
> I'm reaching out from MISKA (miskahealth.in) — clinic-formulated hair fall line from Bangalore: rosemary oil with biotin/caffeine, treatment shampoo, Redensyl serum.
>
> Happy to share high-res product assets, ingredient one-pagers, or a short expert quote on rosemary + peptide routines for your readers.
>
> No obligation — useful if you're updating hair fall roundups.
>
> [Name]

### Guest post pitch (health/beauty blogs)

Subject: Guest post: treatment shampoo vs regular — what changes at the follicle

> Hi [Name],
>
> I'd like to contribute an educational piece (no promotional fluff) on why treatment shampoos deposit actives during the wash window — relevant for your audience dealing with hair fall.
>
> I can include original diagrams or cite supplier data on rosemary/caffeine. Link to our journal post or ingredient page as reference only.
>
> Word count: 900–1200. Let me know your guidelines.

### HARO / journalist queries

Respond only when query matches hair/scalp/beauty India. Lead with facts, mention MISKA once in bio line.

## Prospecting sources (India)

- HealthKart Magazine, Femina, Vogue India beauty
- Dermatology-adjacent blogs (guest posts)
- "Best shampoo for hair fall" affiliate sites — offer honest sample + specs
- Reddit r/IndianSkincareAddicts — value-first comments (no spam); journal links when genuinely helpful

## Quality bar

| Accept | Reject |
|--------|--------|
| DR 20+ relevant site | Link farms, PBNs |
| Editorial context | Paid links without rel=sponsored |
| Ingredient/education anchor | Exact-match spam anchors |
| Google News / health pubs | Foreign irrelevant directories |

## Weekly quota

- 5 new prospects logged
- 2 follow-ups on `contacted`
- 1 new `live` link per month = good progress early stage
